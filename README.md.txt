# MGRUS Logistic SRL

## European Freight Forwarding & Transport Coordination

MGRUS Logistic SRL is a Romania-based freight forwarding and logistics coordination company, specializing in efficient road transport solutions across Europe.

We provide reliable and flexible transport management services through a strong network of trusted carriers.

---

## 🚛 Core Services

- Full Truck Load (FTL) transport across Europe  
- Less Than Truck Load (LTL) solutions  
- International road freight coordination  
- Subcontracting & carrier management  
- Express and time-critical deliveries  
- Spot market transport solutions
